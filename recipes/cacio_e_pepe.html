Cacio e Pepe

Description

It's made with cheese and pepper.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Make the pasta
  2. Mix starchy pasta water with ground pepper
  3. Mix pepper-starch-water with grated cheese
  4. Drain pasta
  5. Quickly whip the starch-cheese-pepper sauce into the pasta
